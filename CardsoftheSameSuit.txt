problem statement: https://www.hackerrank.com/challenges/s10-mcq-6
solution: Brute Force

Bag 1 (R - 3, B - 4) = Total 7 balls.

1. First drawing ball will be red. So, the total ball reduces to 6
2. Next drawing ball should be blue.
3. Blue ball remains 4, total ball 6. So, The probability od second ball will be blue = 4/6 = 2/3
